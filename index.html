About

My work lives where memory distorts into sound. Influenced by latinidad, psychedelia, and industrial noise, I build from fragments—rhythm, language, emotion— shaping worlds that feel intimate, uncanny, and alive.

I'm drawn to the spaces between genres: where distortion softens into melody, where ritual becomes rhythm, and where identity cracks open into something new.

Everything I make is part of a larger conversation— with memory, place, and transformation.
